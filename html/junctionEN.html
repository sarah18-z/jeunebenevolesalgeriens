Description

Technology is advancing rapidly and today more than ever we must be more than informed, we must be at the heart of technological modernization, that is why C-Tech was created.
A scientific event focused mainly on information and communication technologies, bringing together more than 700 participants from different activity sectors who can all contribute in technological development. C-Tech is more than a simple conference, it is a space for exchange and dialogue that allows to discuss innovation, progress and technologies with quality experts who share their time and knowledge for apprenticeship of young talents.
